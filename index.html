Our Methodology

1. Dynamic Spectrum (DS)

Analyzes image intensity to highlight potential tumor regions (ROI) with a distinct color, adaptable to various tumor types.

2. EDWIN Algorithm

The Expanding Dynamic-Window (EDWIN) algorithm adaptively expands and refines the detected ROI to reduce false negatives.

3. Swin Transformer

Extracts multi-scale hierarchical features using efficient shifted window attention for robust contextual understanding.

4. CNN Verification

A lightweight CNN acts as a final verifier to confirm the presence of a tumor and provide the final classification.
